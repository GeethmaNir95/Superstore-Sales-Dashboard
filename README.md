# Superstore Sales Dashboard
## Overview
This project focuses on delivering the key insights and measures of the  sales performance of Superstore during the period from 2014-2017.The project aims to find out the key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Total Sales, Profit Percentage (%) across all time and the Total Quantity of goods sold as well as analysis of profit and  sales across different geographical locations, segements, product categories and sub categories. 

The dashboard includes visualisations highlighting:
 - Key Performance Indicators
     1. Total Sales
     2. Profit Percentage (%) Across All Time
     3. Total Quantity of Goods Sold

 - Line charts depicting trend of Sales Over Time
 - Bar Chart (Orange Color): Sum of Profit showing Product Category and Product Sub-Category
   Key Insights:
      - Copiers product sub-category is the most profitable cateogry with over 50K Total Profit during the period from 2014-  2017.
